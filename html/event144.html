Summary and Details

Eat Local Challenge

Start Time: 2011-09-27 11:00:00
End Time: 2011-09-27 02:00:00
Summary: Once a year, Bon Appétit Management Company issues an ¿Eat Local Challenge¿ to all of our chefs. On Tuesday, September 27th, come to the Great Hall, Marketplace or Fuqua's Fox Center and enjoy a lunch that is made completely of ingredients from within a 150 mile radius of Duke. This act, while seemingly simple, has far reaching implications. To read more about the importance of purchasing local food: www.circleofresponsibility.com
