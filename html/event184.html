Rosh Hashanah - Jewish Holiday

Start Time: 2011-09-28 06:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-30 06:30:00
Summary: The Jewish holiday of Rosh Hashanah begins at sundown on Wednesday, September 28th and concludes at sundown on Friday, September 30th. For a full schedule of Rosh Hashanah meals and services sponsored by Jewish Life at Duke, please visit http://www.studentaffairs.duke.edu/jewishlife. To learn more about Rosh Hashanah, please visit http://www.myjewishlearning.com/holidays/RoshHashana.htm.
