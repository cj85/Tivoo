Summary and Details

Krzyzewskiville Boot Camp

Start Time: 2011-09-27 06:00:00
End Time: 2011-09-27 07:00:00
Summary: Let Duke Personal Training whip you into shape this fall at Krzyzewskiville Boot Camp. Tuesday and Thursday Nights 6 PM 9/13 - thru 10/6 Our drill sergeant will challenge you with cardio drills and strength training exercises. We'll shape you up this school year. Only $64 for all 8 session or $10 to drop in. We accept cash, check and flex on the day of class. If you would like to pre-register for the series with a credit card, contact mdobbins.duke.edu in advance. Program is available to all Duke students as well as faculty/staff members of the gym. Contact Mary Ann at 613-7516 with questions or to pre-register
