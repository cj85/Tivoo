Duke Law Democrats Kick-Off

Start Time: 2011-09-28 12:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-28 01:30:00
Summary: This is our first meeting of a jam-packed year of Democratic activities. Join us for a brief organizational meeting where we'll discuss major goals for supporting candidates and advancing Democratic values in 2011 - 2012. We'll also be electing officers to the exec board, which is open to all class years. Lunch will be served. For more info contact Andrew Jennings, andrew.jennings@duke.edu.
