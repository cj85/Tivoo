Summary and Details

Winners of the 2011 Daylight/CDS Photo Awards Exhibition

Start Time: 2011-09-19 09:00:00
End Time: 2011-12-22 07:00:00
Summary: A solo show featuring Project Prize winner Tamas Dezso and a group exhibition featuring Work-in-Process Prize winner David Pace along with Jurors' Pick winners in both categories. The exhibition will be on view in the Porch and University Galleries at the Center for Documentary Studies at Duke University. Reception: Tuesday, October 4, 6-8 p.m.
