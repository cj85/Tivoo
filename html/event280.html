Summary and Details

NC American Chemical Society Conference

Start Time: 2011-09-30 05:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-30 08:30:00
Summary: The NC ACS Conference will be held at the NC State College of Textiles Centennial Campus in Raleigh, NC at 5:30pm on September 30. The Distinguished Lecturer is Dr. Nicholas Oberlies from the Dept. of Medicinal Chemistry and Pharmacognosy at UNC Greensboro. Poster title submissions for this conference are due September 23. Visit http://tinyurl.com/ncacs125Conference for more information and to sign up.
