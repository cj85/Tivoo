Summary and Details

The First Annual Back-to-School Engagement featuring: Jeff Johnson

Start Time: 2011-09-28 06:45:00
End Time: 2011-09-28 08:15:00
Summary: Duke NAACP presents: The Back-to-School Engagement featuring: JEFF JOHNSON! In collaboration with Duke BSA, NPHC, and several other groups on campus, we are bringing Jeff Johnson, a widely acclaimed journalist, MSNBC and BET commentator, and social activist to engage the Duke Community in a powerful message about transformative leadership and cultural integrity. Please join us for this event!! Catered Reception will precede the start of the program. ALL ARE WELCOME!! SEE YOU THERE!!
