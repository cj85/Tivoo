Economic and Doing Business Reforms: Georgia 2004-2007

Start Time: 2011-09-29 05:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-29 07:00:00
Summary: Aleksi Aleksishvili is a 2004 graduate of the Master of International Development Policy program at the Sanford School of Public Policy. He returned to Georgia to become the Minister of Economy (2004-2005) followed by being appointed Minister of Finance of Georgia from 2005-2007. He is currently Chairman of the Board for the Policy and Management Consulting Group (PMCG).
