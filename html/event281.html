Summary and Details

Shabbat with Jewish Life at Duke - Reform & Conservative Services and Benenson Family Shabbat Dinner

Start Time: 2011-09-30 06:15:00
End Time: 2011-09-30 08:15:00
Summary: Celebrate Shabbat with student-led Reform and Conservative services and enjoy a delicious FREE dinner made possible by the generosity of the Benenson Family. Services begin at 6:15p and are followed by a family-style dinner at 7:15p every week during the academic year that school is in session. To guarantee your spot at dinner, please RSVP at http://www.studentaffairs.duke.edu/jewishlife/shabbat-dinner-form by 12noon on Friday.
