Language Tables Kick-off

Start Time: 2011-09-29 12:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-29 01:30:00
Summary: The International Law Society (ILS) is starting its 2011-12 Language Table program. For more information, please contact Kyle Jackson at kyle.jackson@duke.edu.
