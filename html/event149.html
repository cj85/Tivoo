Summary and Details

Ciompi Quartet Lunchtime Classics #1

Start Time: 2011-09-27 12:00:00
End Time: 2011-09-27 01:00:00
Summary: HAYDN: String Quartet No. 1 in E-flat, Op. 20, No. 1 SHOSTAKOVICH: String Quartet No. 1 in C Major, Op. 49 ### In this special lunchtime series of performances, the Ciompi explores the connections between Haydn and Shostakovich. Each event lasts approximately 50 minutes and features quartets by both composers. Admission is free. Though food is not provided, audience members are encouraged to bring lunch. All concerts begin at noon in the Gothic Reading Room at Perkins Library.
