Summary and Details

Durham Gardening for Yankees

Start Time: 2011-09-30 02:00:00
End Time: 2011-09-30 04:00:00
Summary: Get great gardening tips for those of you new to gardening in Zone 7B. Durham County Extension Master Gardener Leanna Murphy Dono - a recent transplant from Ohio - will share her perspective and expertise on Durham soils, seasons, and a few 'new-to-you' plants to consider as you put down roots in the Triangle. Free (registration required). Duke Gardens, 420 Anderson St.
