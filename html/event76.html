Summary and Details

An Inner View of Interviews

Start Time: 2011-09-26 06:30:00
End Time: 2011-09-26 07:30:00
Summary: This program will provide in-depth summaries of the various assessment tools used by recruiting organizations to assess candidate skill levels. Topics covered will include behavioral interviews, case interviews, cognitive testing, and role play/simulation exercises. Demonstrations will be provided. The presentation will also feature general interview tips designed to give you a competitive edge in your job search. Space is limited!
