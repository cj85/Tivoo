Summary and Details

Chemistry Department Seminar

Start Time: 2011-09-27 11:40:00
End Time: 2011-09-27 12:40:00
Summary: Fernando A. Escobedo, Professor at Cornell University, School of Chemical & Biomolecular Engineering, will present a seminar on Tuesday, September 27, 2011 at 11:40 a.m. in Room 2237 of the French Family Science Center. The title of the seminar is "Entropy-driven Order-disorder Transitions and Self-assembly in Colloidal Systems of Spherical and Non-spherical Particle Shapes". Refreshments will be served before the seminar. This seminar is hosted by Professor Patrick Charbonneau.
